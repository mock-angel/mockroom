About Me

    THINGS TO KNOW ABOUT ME

    I have been interested in photography and editing pictures since I was in grade eight. Back then, I used to capture using my mobile.

    As days passed my passion towards photography grew. When I came to grade eleven, one of my friends had a DSLR and I was fascinated by it. He started capturing moments of people around him, but there was no one to capture his. I was glad to lend my helping hand to him. He then taught me how pictures could be captured from a DSLR and in some tries, I was able to click amazing pictures.

    That's when I realized how good I was in capturing pictures and I started exploring things around me. On 10th March, 2019 my mom gifted me a DSLR and I was on cloud nine. I started clicking pictures from my NIKON D5600.

    Now, along with being a photographer, I am also a digital artist. When it comes to photography, I enjoy capturing moments. I love when I freeze a moment of someone's life forever.
